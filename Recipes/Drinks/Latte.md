## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 2 oz espresso


## Directions

1. Make espresso.

2. Microwave milk in 1 min intervals until warm.

3. Pour espresso in glass.

4. Froth milk until 50% aerated.

5. Pour hot milk until 1 inch left on top.

6. Tilt glass to pour foam.
