## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 2 oz expresso


## Directions

1. Make expresso

2. Microwave milk in 1 min intervals until warm

3. Pour expresso in glass

4. Froth milk until 50% aeriated 

5. Pour hot milk until 1 inch left on top

6. Tilt glass to pour foam
